iOS 5 Features

The free iOS 5 Software Update brings more than 200 new features to iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.

Notification Center

All your alerts in one place. Swipe down from the top of any screen to see all your notifications. View stocks, weather, and more at a glance.

iMessage

Send unlimited text messages via Wi-Fi or 3G from your iPad, iPhone, or iPod touch. Share photos, videos, locations, and contacts with other iOS users.

Newsstand

A custom newsstand for all your magazine and newspaper subscriptions. Automatically updates with new issues in the background.

Reminders

A better way to manage to-dos with location-based reminders. Get alerts when you arrive at or leave specific locations.

Twitter Integration

Tweet directly from Safari, Photos, Camera, YouTube, or Maps. Sign in once and share from anywhere in iOS.

Camera

Access Camera right from the Lock screen. Use volume-up button as a shutter. Grid lines and pinch-to-zoom for better composition.

Photos

Edit photos right on your device. Crop, rotate, enhance, and remove red-eye without leaving the Photos app.

Safari

Safari Reader displays articles without ads or clutter. Reading List saves articles to read later. Tabbed browsing on iPad.

PC Free

Set up and activate your iOS device wirelessly. Download software updates over the air. Back up to iCloud automatically.

iOS 5 Compatibility

iPhone: iPhone 3GS, iPhone 4, iPhone 4S
iPad: iPad, iPad 2
iPod touch: 3rd generation, 4th generation
